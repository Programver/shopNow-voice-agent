ShopNow Shipping and Delivery FAQ

Standard delivery:
- Delivered within 4-7 business days across India.
- Free shipping on orders above Rs 499.
- Shipping charge of Rs 49 for orders below Rs 499.

Express delivery:
- Available in metro cities — Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata.
- Delivered within 1-2 business days.
- Additional charge of Rs 99.

Order tracking:
- Tracking link sent via SMS and email after dispatch.
- Track on ShopNow app or website using order ID.
- Customer support can also provide live tracking updates.

Delivery partners:
- Delhivery, BlueDart, Ekart, and India Post for remote areas.
- Delivery partner and tracking ID shared after dispatch.

Failed delivery:
- Three delivery attempts made before returning to warehouse.
- Customer notified via SMS before each attempt.
- Rescheduling available by calling support.
- Order returned to warehouse if all attempts fail — full refund issued.

Delivery to remote areas:
- India Post used for pin codes not serviced by standard partners.
- May take up to 10-12 business days.
- Free shipping regardless of order value for remote areas.

Cash on delivery:
- Available on orders up to Rs 5000.
- Not available for express delivery.
- COD charge of Rs 25 applies.

International shipping:
- Not available currently.
- ShopNow delivers within India only.
