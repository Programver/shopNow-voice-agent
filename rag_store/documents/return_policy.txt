ShopNow Return and Refund Policy

Eligibility:
- Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery.
- Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging.
- Items marked as non-returnable at time of purchase are not eligible.
- Damaged or defective items are eligible for return regardless of the 7 day window.

How to initiate a return:
- Customer must call ShopNow support or use the app.
- Provide order ID and reason for return.
- A pickup will be scheduled within 2 business days.
- Refund is processed within 5-7 business days after pickup.

Refund methods:
- Original payment method for card and UPI payments.
- ShopNow wallet credit for cash on delivery orders.
- Bank transfer available on request for COD orders.

Non-returnable items:
- Innerwear and swimwear
- Perishable goods
- Customized or personalized products
- Digital products and gift cards

Damaged or wrong items:
- Customer must report within 48 hours of delivery.
- Photo evidence required via app or WhatsApp.
- Full refund or replacement offered.
- No need to return damaged items in most cases.

Exchange policy:
- Size and color exchanges allowed within 7 days.
- Subject to availability.
- Free exchange for first request per order.
