ShopNow Payment FAQ

Accepted payment methods:
- UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM)
- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
- Net banking
- ShopNow wallet
- Cash on delivery (orders up to Rs 5000)
- EMI available on orders above Rs 2000 via select banks

Payment failure:
- If payment fails but amount is deducted, it will be refunded within 5-7 business days.
- Check with your bank if refund is not received after 7 days.
- Retry payment using a different method if issue persists.
- Contact support with your order ID and transaction ID for faster resolution.

Double charge:
- Rare but can happen due to network issues.
- Contact support immediately with both transaction IDs.
- Second charge reversed within 3-5 business days.
- ShopNow does not retain double charged amounts.

EMI:
- Available on credit cards from HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Axis, Kotak.
- Tenure options of 3, 6, 9, and 12 months.
- No cost EMI available on select products.
- Processing fee may apply depending on bank.

ShopNow wallet:
- Loaded via UPI, card, or net banking.
- Used for instant checkout.
- Refunds credited to wallet within 24 hours.
- Wallet balance cannot be transferred to bank account.

GST and invoices:
- GST included in displayed price.
- Invoice available in app under order details.
- Business invoice with GSTIN available on request.
